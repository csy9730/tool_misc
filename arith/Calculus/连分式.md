$$
(1+x)^2=2\\
1+2x+x^2=2\\
x(x+2)=1\\
x= \frac{1}{2+x}\\
x = \frac{1}{2+\frac{1}{2+x}}\\
$$

x= \frac{C-1}{2+x}\\
x = \frac{C-1}{2+\frac{C-1}{2+x}}\\
